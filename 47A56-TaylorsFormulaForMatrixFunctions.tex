Let $p$ be a polynomial and suppose $\mv{A}$ and $\mv{B}$ commute, i.e. $\mv{A}\mv{B}=\mv{B}\mv{A}$, then
\begin{displaymath}
p(\mv{A}+\mv{B})=\sum_{k=0}^{n}\frac{1}{k!}p^{(k)}(\mv{A})\mv{B}^k .
\end{displaymath}
where $n=\deg(p)$.
